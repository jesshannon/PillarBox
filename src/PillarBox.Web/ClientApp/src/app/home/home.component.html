Welcome to PillarBox

Your own private post office.

A mock SMTP server, for testing emails sent from websites and apps during development.

  • Runs on your own computer or local network
  • Supports Windows, Mac and Linux
  • Emails appear instantly with no delay, they never even leave your computer
  • Automatically sorts emails coming from different projects or release stages into separate inboxes
  • Preview emails on simulated devices and screen sizes
  • Easily forward emails on to other testing services such as Litmus
  • Integrate with UI testing frameworks like Selenium, fetch content directly from the email, such as a verification link

PillarBox is designed for individual developers, teams or software testers.

It's a simple lightweight SMTP server running on your computer that catches all emails sent to it. With an easy to use web interface for viewing and testing emails.

When testing your website or application, put in the address of your computer running PillarBox instead of your usual SMTP server. For example "localhost".

Use the SMTP username field to name your project or release stage, and have all the emails automatically organised into folders.

Use slashes in the username to create sub folders like "My Project/Development/Phase1"
